1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use Life Expectancy Calculator (the "App"). Your privacy is our top priority, and we want you to feel confident using our app. This App is designed for generating contact lists and does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

2. Information Collection and Use

  • No Personal Information Collected We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, including but not limited to names, contact information, or usage data.
  • No Device Information Collected The App does not collect any information from your device, such as location data, IP addresses, or device identifiers.

3. Data Security

Since no data is collected or stored by the App, we do not maintain databases or storage locations for user data. The App is designed for offline use, and therefore, it does not transmit any data over the internet.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Your continued use of the App after any changes will constitute your acceptance of such changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the App's practices, please contact us at:

okporiakumiracle@gmail.com

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general use and does not specifically target children under the age of 13. As we do not collect any personal information, we comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

7. Offline Functionality

The App is designed to function entirely offline and does not require an internet connection. All generated contact data is stored locally on your device without any form of upload or external storage.

8. Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
